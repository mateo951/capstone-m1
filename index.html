Let's start with Challengers. Regions around the world will compete in Challengers events in three stages throughout the year. Each stage features two Challengers main events, which any team from the region can qualify for through open qualifiers. At the main events, teams compete for a place in the Challenger qualifiers. Teams that make it to and win the Challengers qualifiers will qualify for a global competition at the Masters events.
